    Poolside Lounging & Stylish Comfort in Bel Air

    Description

    Located within striking distance of coveted Downtown Phoenix and Old Town Scottsdale, this gorgeous vacation rental proves ideal for a family retreat or a getaway with friends. And with a large, private swimming pool and a showroom-quality aesthetic throughout, this property is loaded not only with recreation possibilities but attractive backdrops for your social media posts as well!

    Neighborhood

    You’ll have no trouble finding options for dining and shopping

    Midtown Phoenix is a trending neighborhood highlighted by lovely, well-preserved historic homes and a thriving cultural and performing arts scene. While you’ll have no problems finding great options for dining and nightlife, downtown Phoenix is within striking distance for world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment.

    Midtown Phoenix offers a wide variety of rental communities within close proximity to major highways, the light rail, and Downtown Phoenix, making it an ideal neighborhood for commuters. Midtown Phoenix also touts exceptional dining options with a slew of cozy cafes, tasty restaurants, and modern eateries located along North Central Avenue and East Camelback Road.
    Shopping opportunities abound near Midtown Phoenix as well, with retail destinations like Camelback Colonnade and Biltmore Fashion Park situated within close proximity. Midtown Phoenix is also nearby plenty of recreational activities at local parks as well as the sprawling Phoenix Mountains Preserve, which is just a short drive away.

    Space

    The functional design and sleek decor blend seamlessly in this impressive three-bedroom/two-bathroom house featuring an airy, open-concept floorplan with tasteful artwork and modern finishes adorning each room. Multiple living rooms offer optimal lounging areas with chic – yet cozy – sofas and chairs. The main living room showcases a rustic floor-to-ceiling natural stone fireplace and flatscreen TV.

    Just off the main room sits a stunning chef’s kitchen built with custom wood cabinetry, generous smooth stone countertops, and a subway tile backsplash. The kitchen comes fully stocked with utensils, plates, and cooking tools to assist with your meal preparation. It also has brand name stainless-steel appliances including an oversized refrigerator, dishwasher, gas stove, and microwave. Enjoy the large kitchen island for additional prep space and casual bar seating. For more formal, sit-down meals,
    the separate dining room offers plenty of space for up to six guests.

    Each bedroom offers fine linens and bedding along with a ceiling fan for optimal climate control. The master bedroom features a king-sized bed, a flatscreen TV, and a serene en suite bathroom with a deep soaking tub, large separate shower area, and double vanity. The second bedroom holds two double beds and has a living room area with a sofa bed and flatscreen TV. The third bedroom has a queen-sized bed.

    Whether you’re in the mood for fun or rejuvenation, this property accommodates both. Take the party outside to the spacious swimming pool area furnished with outdoor cushioned sofas, tables, and lounge chairs ideal for sunbathing. For indoor fun, the home includes a game room with a ping pong table and flatscreen TV. There are also multiple desk areas around the house for writing or for working on your laptop.

    Transit

    The Valley Metro light rail offers a convenient and reliable option for public transportation and there are many bicycle routes available in the immediate area. The Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport is less than 9 miles away.

    Notes

    The house is pet-friendly. No more than two pets per stay. There is an extra fee if you would like to bring your furry friends. Ask us about the pet fee and we will send you the details.
